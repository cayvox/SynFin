04 — Confidentiality

Each venue sees
only its own leg.

Synfin settles the split so every venue verifies and executes its own leg without seeing the others. The executor coordinates the whole atomic transaction; each venue's view is compartmentalized — and with no public mempool, there is no front-running surface.

Per-leg confidentiality ≠ anonymity to your own counterparty. A venue does not see other venues' legs; it still sees its own.
